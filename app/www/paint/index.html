How to use

  • Pick a color from the dots
  • Pick a brush size; S is tiny, XL is chunky
  • Tap the pink eraser to erase
  • ↩ / ↪ to undo and redo strokes
  • Tap + to open the menu
  • 💾 saves to Photos (tap 3 times)
  • 🗑️ clears the canvas (tap 3 times so your child won't clear on an accidental tap)
  • Lock app pins the screen so your kiddo won't get into the rest of your phone. Tap the button 4 times quickly to unlock.
